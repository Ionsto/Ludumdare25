This is my ludum dare compo Entery.

Roket!

You play as a contractor hired out by walmart to
destroy hospitals and orphanages for 'developing'.

The controls are 

A - D to move camara;

E - Q to rotate Launcher.

R to place a rocket - you only have a limited supply!

F to launch all the unlaunched rockets.

Dragers - these you drag up and down.

The thrust is speed of acceleration.
The Fuel is how long it accelerates for.
The Ceiling is the leval that the rocket starts spining from.

The aim of the game is to get all the bricks below a certain height.
